About This Website

This is the blog to record and share what I thought/saw/learned and anything happened in my life.

You might be curious about why this blog uses "backtrace.top" as its domain. When I wrapped my head around choosing a domain, I looked around to see if there's anything that can give me some insights, and right at that moment I saw the program got stopped at a breakpoint in Xcode, with the backtrace shown in the left panel. It inspired me that the backtrace is just like a list of posts. The latest function call locates on the top, as the newest post would be shown on the top of list in my blog.

Hopefully the domain would be permanent so that I don't need to change the above definition.
